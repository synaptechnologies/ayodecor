Fluted Panels

Transform your walls with our elegant fluted panels, available in a variety of materials including wood, WPC, and bamboo charcoal. These panels add depth, texture, and sophistication to any space, creating stunning accent walls that become the focal point of your interior design.

Materials & Options

  • Wood Fluted Panels: Natural wood panels that bring warmth and organic beauty to your space. Available in various wood species and finishes.
  • WPC Fluted Panels: Wood-Plastic Composite panels that offer the look of wood with enhanced durability and moisture resistance.
  • Bamboo Charcoal Panels: Eco-friendly panels made from bamboo charcoal, offering a unique dark finish with natural antibacterial properties.

Benefits of Fluted Panels

  • Adds texture and visual interest to plain walls
  • Creates a sense of depth and dimension
  • Enhances acoustics by reducing echo
  • Durable and long-lasting
  • Easy to maintain and clean
  • Can be installed vertically or horizontally for different effects

Applications

Our fluted panels are perfect for:

  • Living room accent walls
  • Bedroom feature walls behind headboards
  • Office reception areas
  • Restaurant and hospitality interiors
  • Retail store displays
  • Hotel lobbies and corridors
