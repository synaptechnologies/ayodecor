3D UV Marble Sheets

Experience the luxury and elegance of natural marble without the hefty price tag and maintenance requirements. Our 3D UV marble sheets offer the stunning beauty of marble with enhanced durability, easy installation, and minimal upkeep.

Features & Benefits

  • Realistic Marble Appearance: Advanced printing technology creates lifelike marble patterns and textures.
  • 3D Effect: Special UV coating creates a three-dimensional effect that adds depth and dimension.
  • Durable & Long-lasting: Resistant to scratches, stains, and moisture, ensuring long-term beauty.
  • Easy Maintenance: Simple to clean with just a damp cloth, no special sealants required.
  • Lightweight: Much lighter than natural marble, making installation easier and more versatile.
  • Cost-effective: Offers the luxurious look of marble at a fraction of the cost.

Applications

Our 3D UV marble sheets are perfect for:

  • Kitchen backsplashes and countertops
  • Bathroom walls and vanity tops
  • Living room feature walls
  • Office reception areas and conference rooms
  • Retail store interiors
  • Hotel lobbies and guest rooms

Available Designs

We offer a wide range of marble designs including:

  • Classic white marble with grey veining
  • Emperador dark brown marble
  • Carrara marble patterns
  • Calacatta gold marble
  • Nero Marquina black marble
  • Custom designs available upon request
